## Current ideas

### [MMX-Info](https://github.com/dzerrenner/mmx-legacy-info)

A data-mining tool for the great game "[Might and Magic X - Legacy](http://might-and-magic.ubi.com/mightandmagicx-legacy/en-gb/home/index.aspx)". The game data is delivered mostly as xml and csv files. The project's goal is to parse that data and transform it into a human readable form.

This will be most likely be a python module to include in your own code.

### RP-Codex

As I'm a passionate pen-and-paper roleplaying gamer, and our party is quite well equipped with modern handheld devices in real life, I seek for a solution to take character sheets and rule hints on a mobile level. 

This will be most likely a full featured web application.
